Simple Baked Chicken Breasts

Description

Simple baked chicken breasts...What's more to say about this recipe? There is sauce to go along with the breasts.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Creole Seasoning
  • Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C.
  3. Rub chicken breasts with oil and season breasts on both sides with salt and Creole seasoning.
  4. Place chicken in broiler pan and bake in oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip the chicken and cook for another 15 minutes, then remove chicken to a plate.
  6. Pour chicken broth into the pan and scrape any browned bits off the pan to create the sauce.
  7. Drizzle the sauce over the chicken and serve.
