Parchment Baked Salmon

Description

Bake your salmon in parchment, which allows the salmon to steam, ensuring deliciously moist fish.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Chopped Basil Leaves
  • Lemon Slices
  • Olive Oil Cooking Spray
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Place salmon fillet skin side down in the middle of large parchment paper.
  3. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
  4. Cut slits into the fillet and stuff the basil leaves into the silts.
  5. Spray fillet with cooking spray and arrange lemon slices on top.
  6. Fold the edges of parchment paper over fillet to seal it into an airtight packet
  7. Place sealed packed onto a baking sheet and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes
  8. To serve, cut open the parchment paper and dig in
